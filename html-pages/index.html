Our story and mission

At the heart of our bakery is Bee—a passionate baker with a love for crafting delicious cakes that bring joy to every celebration. What began as a shared dream between Bee and a dear friend has grown into a beloved bakery known for its creativity, warmth, and dedication to excellence. Though Bee’s friend has since passed on, their spirit and vision live on in every cake we create. With each recipe, Bee continues to honor the bond they shared, ensuring that the original mission—to spread happiness through the art of baking—remains at the core of everything we do. Today, our bakery stands as a testament to friendship, passion, and perseverance. As long as Bee is here, the dream will continue to flourish—one cake at a time.
